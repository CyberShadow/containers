std.allocator.AllocatorWithStats.bytesContracted
@propertyulong bytesContracted() @property const
Read-only properties enabled by the homonym chosen by the user.

Example

       AllocatorWithStats!(Mallocator,
           Options.bytesAllocated | Options.bytesDeallocated) a;
       auto d1 = a.allocate(10);
       auto d2 = a.allocate(11);
       a.deallocate(d1);
       assert(a.bytesAllocated == 21);
       assert(a.bytesDeallocated == 10);
